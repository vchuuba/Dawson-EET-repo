While the conscript can be cheaply massed and the initiate specialized to go against it's peers, the defining trait of the G.I is that it can be defensively deployed in order to switch to it's more powerful sandbag and machinegun combo at the cost of being stationary. This ability makes this unit good against early rushes from more aggressive players, but the need to deploy in order to do any amount of significant damage limits it's range of operations to one of support than as a frontline unit.
